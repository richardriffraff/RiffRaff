Riff Raff Development Handbook

The quality of our work is extremely important to us, not only in terms of pixel-perfect rendition, but also in terms of the security and integrity of our code. A typical Riff Raff web project revolves around a huge amount of traffic and user activity, handling complex data and monetary transactions that need to be robust and secure.

Due diligence is paramount to our planning and architectural procedures, to ensure that our own exacting standards are met before a project is deployed to the public domain. This benchmarking is used to assess a number of criteria, including security, stability and performance.

In order to protect the integrity of our work, and our reputation as a company, we have compiled our own development handbook. This handbook outlines a strict benchmark in security, performance and scalability that all of our work must exceed, and around which our framework itself evolves. It acts as a mantra to our developers, and a guiding light to new members of the team.
